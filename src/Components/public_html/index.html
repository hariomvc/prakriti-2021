About Prakriti

About Us

Prakriti is an initiative by the students of Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, to amalgamate the academia and industry associated with the agricultural and food sector.



History

With its inception in 2010, Prakriti pioneered the cause of organizing an Agri and Food Innovation Fest at a national level. The first edition of Prakriti saw a huge participation from both the academic and the industrial front. Association with renowned companies like ITC, John Deere, Tetrapak and Siemens contributed significantly in catalyzing its success and helped it achieve greater heights. This year, we hope to make the fest bigger and better by extending our heartiest welcome to many more agro-based institutions and enterprises, and coalesce it with the concept of sustainability.

DATA ANALYTICS

Data Analytics is based upon analysing past information to predict future conditions and plan accordingly.

DATA ANALYTICS

Event Description

In the agriculture sector, farmers and agribusiness have to make innumerable decisions every day and intricate complexities involve the various factors influencing them. An essential issue for agricultural planning intention is the accurate yield estimation and estimation of nutrients concentration for the numerous crops involved in the planning or making decisions like which crop to plant before the next harvest. In making these decisions past information is very crucial. Data analysis is based on analyzing past information to predict future conditions in order to make more informed decisions and plan accordingly.

CLICK PRAKRITI

Event Description

Prakriti 2020 is based on the theme of International Year of Plant Health. Participants are expected to use their expertise behind the lens to capture the pictures which speak volumes about your thought process behind it.
According to Food and Agricultural Organization, 40% of food crops are lost to pests and diseases leading to critical shortages of food in some parts of the world.
Adhering to this theme, a total of 4 photographs are to be submitted per individual/team:
Healthy Plants – 2 photographs
Click photographs that depicts the natural beauty of plants and glorifies their importance as the supporting pillars of human life on earth.

Unhealthy plants : 2 photographs
Click photographs of plants that have shredded growth showing the various constraints it might be facing eg. improper environmental conditions,insect attack,damage by humans etc.

Rules

  • 1) Participants who have registered online would be able to participate.
  • 2) Participants need to bring their own professional cameras/android phones, pendrives, data cables, chargers and other requirements.
  • 3) Participants can take part in this event either individually or a team of max 3 members.
  • 4) The participants need to click the relevant images on two days (20th & 21st march) in our campus itself
  • 5) The participants need to submit their entries in two parts ,firstly the soft copy of the pictures clicked should be mailed at contact@prkrt.co.in and secondly the participants need to take out the printout of all the 4 pictures to be displayed in open exhibition on the third day of the fest(22nd of March). Both these submissions should be done before 11:55 pm, 21st March,2020.
  • 6) If at any point it is found that the images have been downloaded from somewhere and have not been clicked in Prakriti, the team/individual will be disqualified.
  • 7) To monitor plagiarism, the managing committee has taken some measures. Participants need to click an additional pic for each of their 4 photographs.This additional pic should contain some distinguishable landmark as background along with the entity on which they are focusing in order to recognize the location of your snapshot.
  • 8) Participants just need to mail these additional images, no hardcopy is required.
  • 9) The mail should consist the name of the individual/team and the college name.
  • 10) The judging criteria would significantly depend on the proximity of the clicked pictures to the theme mentioned above.
